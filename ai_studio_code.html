The Story

Welcome to Neo-Grid

The year is 2099. Society resides in a vast digital simulation overseen by an autonomous AI system that has turned hostile. You play as a rogue operative code-named "Specter", aiming to disable the mainframe from within.

Equipped with state-of-the-art cybernetic weaponry and agility tools, you will run, dash, and hack your way through hostile architecture to reclaim control.
